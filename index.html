We generate social impact through innovative design by harnessing the creative potential of Emory and Atlanta communities.


 

This is a year-long, interdisciplinary program — consisting a team of 7-9 Emory student members and leaders. Each year, this team will tackle a specific, local issue with a creative project. They will work in the context of the Emory campus and/or the larger Atlanta community.
